Meet Our Team

Lora began her career 12 years ago with a sincere passion for style and genuine care for each individual's needs. In order to fine-tune her natural talent, she felt it essential to continue her path onwards with an accelerated technical education. She is honors graduate and has successfully earned diplomas in Classic as well as Modern Colour and Cutting, Creative Design and Artistic Up- Styling Programs. Working closely and training with industry specialists from Aveda, Vidal Sassoon, Schwarzkopf, Bumble and Bumble, Label M and Davines has helped her to develop balance and harmony.

Since 2011 I have had the pleasure of working in the one field that I love, makeup artistry. My makeup career began five years ago as a beauty advisor for Sears Canada’s Cargo Cosmetics. One year later I joined the Mac Cosmetics’ Hudson's Bay Rideau team as one of their Mac Artists. While working for Mac I received certifications for Makeup Application, Makeup Lessons and Advanced Makeup Certifications. I have worked as a freelance makeup artist, where I have worked on commercial events, such as fashion shows, editorial publications, and makeup workshops.

Suzan started her career 9 years ago in Russia. Her passion and kind nature provide an aura that is hard to miss. Suzan has a diploma from the Academy of Coloristics and Geometry in Moscow. Her cutting technique is influenced by Alexander Todchuk, a renowned Russian hairstylist. She loves working with all of the Davines products and is always educating her clients on how to keep healthy and beautiful hair. Irina believes the best approach in providing a style that is fitting for each guest is to take in to account all contributing factors; texture, structure and physical features.
